## Purpose of the Study

You are being asked to participate in a research study about smartphone usage patterns. The purpose of this study is to understand how people use their smartphones and how awareness of usage patterns affects behavior over time.

## What You Will Be Asked to Do

If you agree to participate, you will:

1. **Install an app** on your Android smartphone
2. **Use your phone normally** for 10 days
3. **Check the app daily** to view your usage statistics when prompted
4. **Allow automatic data collection** including:
   - Total daily screen time
   - Time spent in each app
   - Number of phone unlocks
   - Number and source of notifications

## Data Collection

### What We Collect:
- ✅ Your name (for payment purposes only)
- ✅ Daily screen time totals
- ✅ App names and usage duration
- ✅ Number of phone unlocks
- ✅ Number of notifications received
- ✅ Apps that send notifications

### What We Do NOT Collect:
- ❌ Notification content or messages
- ❌ Phone calls or contacts
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Photos, videos, or files
- ❌ Passwords or sensitive information
- ❌ What you do inside apps
- ❌ Websites you visit

## Data Storage and Privacy

- Your data will be stored securely in a password-protected Google Sheet
- Only the research team will have access to the data
- Your name will be replaced with an anonymous ID for data analysis
- Your name will only be used to track study completion for payment
- Data will be retained for [X years] for research purposes
- Published results will not identify individual participants

## Time Commitment

- **Initial setup**: 5-10 minutes
- **Daily time**: 2-3 minutes to check your stats
- **Total duration**: 10 consecutive days

### Potential Risks:
- **Minimal risk** - similar to normal app usage
- Minor battery drain from background tracking

### Potential Benefits:
- Gain awareness of your smartphone usage habits
- See detailed statistics about your behavior
- Contribute to scientific understanding of technology use

## Voluntary Participation

Your participation is **completely voluntary**. You may:
- Choose not to participate
- Withdraw at any time without penalty
- Skip daily check-ins (though it's encouraged)
- Uninstall the app at any time

If you withdraw before completing 10 days, you will not receive compensation, but any data collected will be deleted if you request it.
